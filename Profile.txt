Sagar R. Kapadia is a researcher and strategic analyst based in Surat, Gujarat (India), working through his firm Cloud Nine Consulting. While his professional background includes significant technical and software engineering experience, his present focus is on uncovering, documenting, and analysing the deeper structures of geopolitical power, ideological conflict, and civilizational continuity.

AREAS OF INTEREST AND FOCUS:

1.	Deep State Dynamics and Global Realignment

Study of transnational deep state architectures, their convergence into a meta-governance structure, and their role in suppressing civilizational autonomy.

2.  Sanatan Dharm as a concealed Imperialist Ideology

Exploration of the fusion between Marxist statism and Islamist theocracy, including its operational methods: NGO warfare, demographic destabilization, and narrative colonization.

4.	India’s Nodal Role

Strategic positioning of India as a civilizational bridge — connecting antagonistic players such as Israel, Iran, Russia, the US, and Central Asia.

5.	Psycho-Civilizational Warfare

Research into mechanisms of mass psychological control, including psychotronic technologies, media subversion, and spiritual disorientation as tools of state and suprastate influence.

6.	Institutional Accountability in India

Investigation of systemic police/judicial abuse and surveillance practices, especially those involving state-sponsored coercion or cover-ups.

7.	Philosophical-Ethical Counter-Narratives

Advocacy for civilizational ethics based on Dharma, Logos, and perennial wisdom as counters to ideological subversion and postmodern nihilism.

8.	Geopolitical Fault Lines Post-2020

Tracking the unravelling of global liberalism and emergence of polarized ideological blocs vying for civilizational supremacy.

8.	Narrative Control and Dystopian Forewarnings

Study of narrative suppression, archival erasure, and media weaponization, with dystopian literature serving as a prophetic lens.

9.	Artificial Intelligence and AGI Governance

Analysis of emerging AI/AGI systems as both tools of liberation and mechanisms of global control — including their intersection with surveillance states, synthetic ideology, and post-human futures.

10.	Weapons of Mass Destruction: Biological, Ecological, and Economic

Examination of silent, deniable, or systemic warfare mechanisms, including biowarfare, engineered pandemics, ecological sabotage, and economic destabilization used for subjugation or reordering.
