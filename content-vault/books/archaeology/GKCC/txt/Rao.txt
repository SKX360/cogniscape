Rao
•	Underwater archaeological methods like sonar, sub-bottom profiling, and remote sampling — considered acceptable globally — are undermined in India due to political bias.
•	Rao stresses that the skepticism is not scientific but ideological, and criticizes how legitimate scholars like Dr. Joshi (a physicist) were derided.
•	He calls attention to:
o	Deliberate suppression of the discovery.
o	Institutional inertia from the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI).
o	A refusal to engage with technological advancements in archaeological methodology.
