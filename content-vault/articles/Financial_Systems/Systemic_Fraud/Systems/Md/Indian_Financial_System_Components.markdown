| Component/Source | Description | Threshold | Mandatory | Penalties for Non-Compliance |
|------------------|-------------|-----------|-----------|------------------------------|
| TDS/TCS Deductors (Employers and Contractors) | Employers (Section 192, Form 24Q) report salary TDS, including breakups and perquisites. Contractors (Section 194C, Form 26Q) report business payment deductions. Integrated into AIS Part B. | Varies: e.g., ₹30,000/year for professional fees (Section 194J); no threshold for salary TDS. | Yes, under Sections 192, 194C, 206C. | ₹200/day for late filing (Section 234E); ₹10,000-₹1,00,000 for non-filing (Section 271H); prosecution for willful failure (Section 276CC). |
| Statement of Financial Transactions (SFT) Reporters | Banks, post offices, stock brokers, mutual funds, and registrars report high-value transactions (e.g., cash deposits > ₹10 lakh under SFT-001, securities via SFT-008/017). Includes stock exchange data via depositories/RTAs. | Varies: e.g., ₹10 lakh for cash deposits/withdrawals (SFT-001/002); ₹1 lakh for credit card payments (SFT-009). | Yes, under Section 285BA. | ₹100-₹500/day for late filing (Section 271FA); ₹50,000 for inaccurate data; prosecution for non-compliance (Section 277A). |
| GST Network | Reports turnover/purchases from GSTR-3B/GSTR-1 via MoU. Includes customs data for imports/exports. Visible in AIS under "Other Information" (e.g., EXC-GSTR3B). | No specific threshold; all registered taxpayers’ data shared. | Yes, via MoU with Income Tax Department. | Penalties under CGST Act (₹10,000-₹25,000 for non-filing GSTR); no direct penalty under Income Tax Act for GST data sharing. |
| Foreign Exchange Dealers | Authorized dealers report outward remittances and forex purchases via Form 15CC or SFT-013, including tour packages (Section 206C(1G)). | ₹10 lakh for remittances under LRS (SFT-013); no threshold for TCS on forex. | Yes, under Section 285BA and FEMA. | ₹100-₹500/day for late SFT filing (Section 271FA); FEMA penalties up to thrice the transaction amount or ₹2 lakh (Section 13, FEMA). |
| Credit Card Issuers | Report payments > ₹1 lakh (individual) or ₹10 lakh (non-individual) annually under SFT-009. Tracks high-value spending. | ₹1 lakh (individuals); ₹10 lakh (others). | Yes, under Section 285BA. | ₹100-₹500/day for late filing (Section 271FA); ₹50,000 for inaccurate data. |
| Property Registrars and ITR Linkages | Registrars report property purchases (SFT-012); seller’s ITR (Schedule CG) links buyer details. Includes customs data for high-value imports via SFT/GST. | ₹30 lakh for immovable property (SFT-012). | Yes, under Section 285BA. | ₹100-₹500/day for late filing (Section 271FA); ₹50,000 for inaccurate data. |
| Other Financial Institutions (e.g., Insurance, PF Trustees) | Report life insurance receipts (Section 194DA), PF withdrawals (Section 192A), interest/dividends. | Varies: e.g., ₹50,000 for PF withdrawals; ₹5,000 for insurance TDS. | Yes, under respective TDS sections. | ₹200/day for late TDS filing (Section 234E); ₹10,000-₹1,00,000 for non-filing (Section 271H). |
| Central Processing System (Directorate of Income Tax Systems) | Validates, deduplicates, and aggregates data for AIS/TIS generation. | Not applicable. | Not applicable (internal system). | Not applicable; internal compliance enforced. |
| E-Filing Portal and Offline Utility | Provides secure, PAN-based access to AIS/TIS for taxpayers. | Not applicable. | Not applicable (user access platform). | Not applicable. |
| Feedback and Correction Mechanism | Allows taxpayers to flag errors, triggering source verification for AIS/TIS updates. | Not applicable. | Optional for taxpayers; mandatory for sources to respond. | No direct penalty for taxpayers; sources face SFT/TDS penalties for non-compliance. |
